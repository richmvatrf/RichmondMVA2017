Market Value Analysis

The Market Value Analysis (MVA) is a tool to assist residents and policymakers identify and understand the elements of their local real estate markets. It is an objective, data-driven tool built on local administrative data and validated with local experts. With an MVA, public officials and private actors can more precisely target intervention strategies in weak markets and support sustainable growth in stronger markets.

The Richmond MVA was completed in 2017 by Reinvestment Fund in partnership with the City of Richmond and the Invest Health RVA team.
